About Us

Chungking Express, Corp. is a U.S.-registered company based in Ithaca, New York. We focus on introducing and distributing advanced service robots for apartment communities, hotels, and restaurants.

Our founding team combines international supply chain expertise and local market knowledge. With strong ties to both Chinese robotics manufacturers and U.S. businesses, we serve as a trusted bridge between innovation and application.

Our Mission

To address everyday delivery and service challenges — from piled-up Amazon packages in apartment lobbies, to secure hotel room service, to high-pressure restaurant environments.
